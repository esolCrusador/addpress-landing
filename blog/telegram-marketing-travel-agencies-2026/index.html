    How to Do Marketing for Travel Agencies on Telegram [2026 Guide]

    Jan 29, 202612 min read

    Telegram is no longer just a messaging app—it has become a powerful marketing channel for businesses. With over 900 million active users worldwide, it's a platform where communities thrive, conversations happen in real time, and brands can directly connect with potential customers. For B2B companies especially, Telegram offers unique opportunities to engage, qualify, and convert leads.

    In this guide, we'll walk you through how to do marketing on Telegram in 2026, and how tools like AddProcess, combined with Kommo CRM, can automate lead qualification to save your sales team hours of manual work.

    Why Telegram Is a Must-Have Marketing Channel in 2026

    Direct Communication

    Unlike email or ads, Telegram gives you instant, two-way communication with prospects. No spam filters, no algorithms.

    Growing Global Audience

    Telegram is widely used in Europe, Asia, and Latin America — fast-growing markets for small and medium businesses.

    High Engagement Rates

    Telegram channels and groups often see open rates of 60–80%, far higher than email campaigns.

    Automation-Friendly

    Telegram bots and integrations make it easy to automate responses, capture data, and qualify leads at scale.

    Telegram Marketing Tactics That Work

    1. Build a Telegram Channel for Thought Leadership

    Share valuable insights, product updates, case studies, and industry news. Position your company as a trusted voice.

    2. Create Niche Groups

    Groups allow direct engagement with your community. Use them for Q&A sessions, onboarding discussions, or product feedback loops.

    3. Use Bots for Engagement

    Bots can deliver quizzes, surveys, and lead forms—perfect for capturing interest without friction.

    4. Promote via Cross-Channels

    Use LinkedIn, email, and paid ads to drive traffic to your Telegram community. Offer exclusive content or incentives for joining.

    Automating Lead Qualification with AddProcess + Kommo

    Here's where things get powerful. Building an audience on Telegram is step one. Step two is turning that audience into qualified leads without overwhelming your sales team.

    That's what AddProcess does.

    • Lead Capture: AddProcess connects with your Telegram bot or group to collect lead details.
    • Smart Qualification: Using predefined criteria (budget, role, company size, etc.), leads are automatically scored.
    • CRM Sync: Qualified leads are sent directly to Kommo, where your sales team can follow up immediately.
    • Real-Time Notifications: Your reps get alerts in Kommo whenever a hot lead comes in.

    This means no more spreadsheets, no more manual sorting—just sales-ready leads from your Telegram marketing funnel.

    Example Workflow

    1. A user sees an ad that leads to a group in Telegram with a chat bot to leave a tour request.
    2. The bot asks qualifying questions (e.g., "Where would you like to go? What's your budget?" etc.).
    3. AddProcess captures responses, scores the lead, and pushes it into Kommo.
    4. Your sales team gets a notification and can engage right away.

    Result? Faster responses, higher conversion rates, and less wasted time.

    Best Practices for Telegram Marketing in 2026

    • Be Human First: Don't just sell—engage in conversations and provide real value.
    • Automate Wisely: Use bots and AddProcess to streamline, but keep the personal touch when closing deals.
    • Respect Privacy: Telegram users value security. Be transparent about how you use their data.
    • Test & Optimize: Track what type of content and engagement drives the best-qualified leads.

    Conclusion

    Marketing on Telegram in 2026 isn't about spamming groups or sending endless promos—it's about building relationships and automating smartly.
