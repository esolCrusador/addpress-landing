    The AddProcess Manifesto

    Our mission is to build connected, efficient and user-centric leads qualification solutions that empower businesses to use cutting-edge technologies and grow faster.

    Why We Built AddProcess

    At AddProcess, we want to help service-based businesses remove pressure from their overworked staff by streamlining booking process. The AI agent works outside office hours, ensuring the company doesn't miss a single client.

    Leads Qualification at Scale

    The more ads you launch, the more unqualified leads you get. At a highly competitive niche this puts a lot of pressure on managers who struggle to address every question in messengers. That's where AddProcess steps in and helps teams become more efficient.

    How?

    Leveraging the power of AI agents and custom workflows helps to make the most from marketing funnels. We have ready-made templates for travel, real estate and other types of businesses.

    Delegate to Go Next Level

    If you get leads during off hours and there is nobody to attend the inquiry you're likely to lose a client. With a chat bot ready to ask the right questions it's easy to convert that lead into a qualified one or even close a sale any time.

    We're here to make customer engagement and growth easier for businesses scaling to the next level. So their teams have more energy and focus to hone their craft, tell their story, and build lasting relationships with their customers.

    That's why we do what we do.
